About me

My first taste of technology was at a young age when my dad brought home his bulky Toshiba work laptop in the mid-90s and dialed into the World Wide Web. Little did he know, this was a defining moment in my young life. From then on, I would spend much of my youth tinkering with computers, soaking in as much as possible.


Flash-forward nearly two decades and I'm still just as fascinated.


After graduating from the University of Florida, I decided to follow my passion for technology and make a career out of it. Today, I work at my alma mater as an IT analyst and full stack developer building enterprise web applications in Python and JavaScript.
